## 6. Knowledge Gaps — Suggested New Sources

Current wiki covers GPT-2-era fundamentals well. Gaps at the frontier:

| Gap | Why It Matters | Suggested Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Rotary Positional Embeddings (RoPE)** | LLaMA, Mistral, and most modern models use RoPE instead of learnable positional embeddings — current [[embeddings]] page only covers GPT-2 style | LLaMA 2 paper or RoPE paper (Su et al. 2021) |
| **RLHF / InstructGPT** | [[fine-tuning]] mentions instruction fine-tuning but the reward model + PPO pipeline is entirely absent | InstructGPT paper (Ouyang et al. 2022) |
| **Grouped Query Attention (GQA)** | LLaMA 2/3 uses GQA to reduce KV cache memory — important for understanding why modern models differ from GPT-2 | LLaMA 2 paper |
| **Mixture of Experts (MoE)** | Mixtral, GPT-4 (estimated) use sparse MoE — not covered at all | Mixtral paper or Switch Transformer |
| **BPE at scale / SentencePiece** | LLaMA uses SentencePiece rather than tiktoken — subtle differences in vocab construction | LLaMA tokenizer docs |
